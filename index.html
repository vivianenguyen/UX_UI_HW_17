She is an empathetic user and UX designer who previously worked in Aerospace Manufacturing with a Business Finance degree. She wants to be part of the evolution of technology and partake in improving the user experience in the modern world. Identifying the problems through UX research, ideating on ideas, and creating prototypes from wireframes allow her to be part of the solution of today’s technological advances. Collaborating with others through brainstorming and ideation gives her the power to impact society with her unique touch on creativity. She hopes to connect all users from various demographics and age groups together to make technology the most user-friendly solution of all human problems.
